This is a **multi-file project**, which the template renders as a single-page
**devlog**: all parts stacked in `order`, with a parts timeline at the top.
Unlike a blog series, project parts are *not* separate pages — they're sections
of `/projects/example-devlog/`.

Put the project-level fields (`featured`, `tag`, `demo`, `source`, `image`) on
part 1; the build treats them as canonical for the card.
